About

Who’s behind GPUcloud.store?

GPUcloud.store is built around a simple idea: high-performance infrastructure should be understandable, negotiable, and aligned with your business goals – not just your benchmarks.

We are cloud consultants with a global Independent Datacenter network powered by Si Exchange.

We sit at the intersection of technical infrastructure, commercial reality, and cross-border networks. That means helping you choose not just the “fastest hardware”, but the architecture and partnerships that actually move your roadmap forward.

With access to a broad network of independent datacenters and GPU providers, we help you navigate capacity, pricing, and reliability while keeping your teams focused on building products — not chasing hardware.
